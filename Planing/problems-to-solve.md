# Dirac Equation Solver

## Problems to Takle
Given that in the usual representation, the Dirac Equation is:
$$
(i \gamma_\mu \partial^\mu - m)\psi = 0
$$
where using natural units and $\psi \in \mathbb{C}^4$, a bispinor, the solver
should be able to deal with this structure that can be either a double complex
double or a column matrix 4x1 with complex entries.
